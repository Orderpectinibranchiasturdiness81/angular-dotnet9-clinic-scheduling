## 🩺 Overview

This application helps clinics and therapy centers manage their daily schedules. It handles patient bookings, stores patient profiles, tracks doctor availability, and sends appointment reminders. The software creates a central hub for clinic staff to organize their day. It runs on your local computer to keep your patient data secure.

## 📋 Features

*   **Online Booking:** Patients see available slots and book appointments through a clean interface.
*   **Patient Profiles:** Keep records of contact details and medical history in one place.
*   **Availability Tracking:** Staff set work hours so the system knows when to open or close slots.
*   **Reminders:** The system notifies patients of upcoming visits to reduce missed appointments.
*   **Admin Dashboard:** See a bird’s-eye view of the clinic operations.
*   **Secure Storage:** Your data stays on your machine, which protects privacy.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting and Support

Use these steps if you encounter an issue during setup or operation:

*   **The application does not open:** Ensure you have the latest version of Windows Updates installed. Restart your computer and try launching the icon again.
*   **The screen looks blank:** Refresh the browser page. If the issue persists, close the application from the system tray near your clock and start it again.
*   **Data does not save:** Check your internet connection. Even though the app runs locally, it needs network access to sync appointment reminders with your patient database.
*   **Software is slow:** Close other applications that use high amounts of memory, such as video editing software or many browser tabs.
